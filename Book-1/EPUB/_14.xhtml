Tejas: Oh, the computer is very clever. Can it do everything?
Moz: A computer can help you do several things. But there are also many things that it cannot do.

You can make fresh fruit juice in your mixer. Can a computer be used like a mixer?
No, It cannot!
You can wash clothes in your washing machine. Can a computer be used like a washing machine?
No, It cannot!

Learning Outcome

After you have studied this lesson, you will be able to:
